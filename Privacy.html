Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to OpenSSL Decryptor. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our web-based tools.

2. Zero-Data Collection (Local Processing)

Unlike many online tools, OpenSSL Decryptor is a client-side application. This means:

  • Your passwords and secret keys are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Your encrypted and decrypted data stay within your browser's memory.
  • All cryptographic computations are performed locally using the Web Crypto API.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use third-party advertising services provided by Google Inc. ("Google") to serve ads when you visit our website. To comply with AdSense policies, please be aware of the following:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

4. Log Files

Like most standard website servers, we may use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks. This data is used purely to analyze trends and administer the site, and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

5. Data Security

Because our tool operates locally, the security of your data depends on the security of your own device. We recommend using the service on a trusted computer and keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

6. Links to Third-Party Sites

Our website contains links to other websites (such as the Terms and Conditions generator). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other sites.
